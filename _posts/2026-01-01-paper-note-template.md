Use posts for longer explanations that should stay more informal than the formal publication list: implementation details, ablations, demos, or a simplified explanation of a paper.

## What this post should contain

- Problem setup and motivation.
- Main idea in plain language.
- Key implementation details.
- Links to the paper page, code, demo, and BibTeX.

This starter post can be replaced once the first real paper note is ready.
